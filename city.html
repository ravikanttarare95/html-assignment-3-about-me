The name of district I live in is Bhandara.

What is Bhandara famous for?
Bhandara is known for its large production of rice. Tumsar, a tahsil town, is a noted rice market. Bhandara town is also known as "Brass City" owing to the presence of a large brass products industry. Bhandara has several tourist destinations, like Ambhora Bridge, Gose Khurd Dam, Ambagad Fort,etc.
