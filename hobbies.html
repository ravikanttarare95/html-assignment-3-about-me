My Hobbies

I love to do so many things but I'm going to explain about my 2 hobbies only.

1. Listening to music

Listening to music is a fun and relaxing way to spend time.

Benefits of listening to music

Stress relief: Listening to music can help reduce stress and anxiety by lowering cortisol levels.

Mood boost: Music can trigger the release of dopamine, which can make you feel more optimistic, joyful, and inspired.

Improved focus: Music can help improve your productivity, attention, and focus.

Better sleep: Listening to music can help you sleep better.

Conversation starter: Music can be a great way to start a conversation with someone who has similar taste in music.

Imagination stimulation: Music can transport you to another time and place, stimulating your imagination.

2. Cooking

My opinion is that cooking is the best hobby to have, because it can be very useful in life. Cooking is my hobby and has been for several years.



How did cooking become my hobby?
I was about 16 years old when I started learning how to cook. Cooking is something that I learnt from my mother. When I was about to enter into 11th standard, I joined a coaching class for 11th & 12th standard. I used to live in a rental room in those days. After some days I started to learn cooking and learned to cook 2-3 vegetables. Those days, I used to ask my mother about recipes & cook every day, and eventually it became my hobby.
But now the main reason I love to cook is because it keeps me stress-free. For example, when I am cooking, I have very little time to think, because the cooking is challenging my mind and taking my mind off the stress of everyday life.
